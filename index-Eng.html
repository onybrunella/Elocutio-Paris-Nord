Elocutio, the floor is yours

Presentation of the day.

The eloquence fair will open its doors for its first edition on June 17, 2022.

We are delighted to share with you this happy event that we have been planning for over 7 months now.
Between learning stage expression, voice management, classical speech methodology and much more...
eloquence will be elected queen of the event !

During this day, you will be completely immersed in the world of rhetoric, straight talk and public speaking with the theme "The Metaverse".

In view of the various challenges of life in society to which communication responds, we invite you to come and experienceduring this day the hidden sources of what Aristotle called rhetoric.

In this first edition of the launch, we have concocted a whole bunch of activities for you that you can do with our various speakers and professionals of the speaking speech.

In the same universe, the day will end with our famous eloquence contest. Some students of the IUT of Villetaneuse will share with you their performances that they have carefully prepared.

Your turn to play your art !

Program of the day.

The university will open its doors from 9:30am to 12pm.

8:30 am to 9:30 am :
Welcome breakfast + opening and presentation of the event.

9:30 am to 3:30 pm :
Opening of the different stands dedicated to the discovery of the fundamentals of eloquence.

3:30 pm to 5:00 pm :
Eloquence contest + Presentation of the different finalists in front of the jury of the contest.

5:00 pm to 5:30 pm :
Closing of the eloquence day and presentation of gifts to the winners of the raffle.

The modalities of the eloquence contest.

  • Each participant has 5 to 10 minutes to speak.
  • Participants do not have the right to a support (paper, phone, etc...).
  • The jury has between 20 minutes and 1 hour to deliberate.
  • Contest participants may use the entire stage.
  • The contest judges will be able to re-watch each participant's performance if necessary.
  • The first three winners of the contest will win a gift certificate of a separate value.
    The one arriving in first position will win a check worth 300 €, the second one worth 150 €, and the third one worth 100 €.
